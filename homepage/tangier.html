Tangier

Tangier was my first port of call travelling wise, after watching the Anthony Bourdain episode based there, I was fixated on paying a visit. In all honesty, It didn't fully live up to expectations. There were some really pictoresque parts - but not an awful lot to see or do. As it's a super touristy destination, It was a bit of shock to the system with all the constant hawkers, that did put a bit of a downer on the experience.

Favourite Parts of Tangier:

  • The Riad hostel, run by really nice staff and set up to easily make pals on the roof terrace
  • Visiting Cafe Baba, a favourite of Keith Richards'
  • Some of the plants spotted- including the birds of paradise pictured
  • Wondering around the medina

Least Favourite parts

  • Not being able to swim in the sea due to pollution
  • Pestery hawkers and unwanted guides
  • A cat being in my bed when I went to go to sleep, setting my allegies off for a few days
