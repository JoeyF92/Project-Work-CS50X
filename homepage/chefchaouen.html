Chefchaouen

Ah Chefchaouen, fave place so far no doubt. I came with the impression that it would be way over hyped and full of instagram influencers. I was completely wrong, this place is beautiful! I wished I'd brought my laptop with me so I could have stayed and worked out here. The people here are so laid back, wondering the streets if so much fun and it's great for hiking in the mountains.

Favourite Parts of Chefchaouen:

  • Finding a spot in the mountains behind the Spanish Mosque for sunset
  • Wondering around all the blue streets

Least Favourite parts

  • Only staying one day!
  • Not being able to see due to the cat allergies
  • Getting told off on the bus home for not buying a ticket and holding everyone up
