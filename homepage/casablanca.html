Casablanca

I'm currently based in Casablanca for the next 3 months. Originally I'd come out to study with Le Wagon, but they decided to cancel the course a week before it commenced..shocker. Anyway, I've decided to carry on self-studying and enjoying being able to do it full-time. I've got my eyes on the next CS50 course which is bit more indepth with Python and Javascript. Potentially will do some front-end design work around that- -and maybe going in more depth with SQL as i've enjoyed that.

Favourite Parts of Casa:

  • The Skate park takes number 1 spot
  • It's not as hassley as other Moroccan cities - the people here are super welcoming
  • Surfing at Ain Diab
  • The Cake shop and pancake stall down the road
  • There's some crazy architecture here - the police station looks like in should be in gotham city
  • The cats eyes on the roads here sparkle
  • Souk Jemiaa

Least Favourite parts

  • The constant car horn sounds here - even at like 5am
  • It's quite ugly here, with not so much to see
  • Finding vegetarian food
